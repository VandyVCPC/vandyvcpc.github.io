About VCPC

Vanderbilt Competitive Programming Club (VCPC) serves as a community for competitive programmers and students who are actively preparing for technical interviews.

To promote the communication and learning of its members, VCPC invites talented students to hold regular training sessions for Leetcode and Competitive Programming.

These sessions expose the logical thinking behind the coding challenges to the students and help them improve their performance both in technical interviews and competitive programming events.
